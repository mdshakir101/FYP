Introduction

"Marketing tool" - a bit of advertising for your FYP system, explaining:
-your project's USP, (e.g., offering features/functions not found or very expensive in competitor systems)
-how it makes your user's job/workflow more convenient, percentage increase in productivity, time savings, etc.
-for FYP projects implementing "multimedia games," advertise your main characters, special weapons/equipment, unique combat system, cinematic trailers, fantastic scenery, etc.
