FYP Topic

Project Description: As digital platforms increasingly shape how individuals buy and sell goods, traditional auction methods are becoming more obsolete due to their dependence on physical location and intermediaries. This setup not only limits the variety of items available but also increases the operational costs for organisers and participants alike, reducing overall efficiency.

Objectives:

  • Design and create an online auction platform: Develop a functional online platform for hosting auctions
  • Real-time bidding capability: Enable users to bid in real time.
  • Support various auction types: Incorporate English, Dutch, and Sealed formats.
